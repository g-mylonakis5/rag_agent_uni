# EUROLEAGUE COMPETITION LOGIC & RULES (SEASON 2025-2026)

## 1. LEAGUE STRUCTURE AND FORMAT
* The EuroLeague consists of 18 professional basketball teams from across Europe.
* Regular Season: 18 teams play in a double round-robin format (one home and one away game), totaling 34 rounds.
* Play-In Showdown: Teams finishing from 7th to 10th place in the regular season compete for the final two playoff spots (7th and 8th seed).
* Playoffs: The top 8 teams compete in a best-of-five series (Quarterfinals).
* Final Four: The four winners of the Quarterfinals progress to a single-elimination tournament consisting of two Semifinals, a Third-place game, and the Championship Final.

## 2. STATISTICAL ABBREVIATIONS (CSV COLUMN MAPPING)
* PTS: Points Scored.
* REB: Total Rebounds (Defensive + Offensive).
* AST: Assists.
* STL: Steals.
* TO: Turnovers (Loss of possession).
* PIR: Performance Index Rating. A metric used to determine a player's overall efficiency.
* 2FG: Two-point Field Goals (Successful shots / Total attempts).
* 3FG: Three-point Field Goals (Successful shots / Total attempts).
* FT: Free Throws (Successful shots / Total attempts).
* MIN: Minutes Played during the game.
* DNP: Did Not Play (used for players who were on the bench but never entered the court).

## 3. PIR (PERFORMANCE INDEX RATING) FORMULA
The PIR is calculated using the following formula:
(Points + Rebounds + Assists + Steals + Blocks + Fouls Drawn) - (Missed Field Goals + Missed Free Throws + Turnovers + Shots Blocked + Personal Fouls Committed).

## 4. TIE-BREAKING RULES (RANKING LOGIC)
If two or more teams have the same win-loss record at the end of the regular season, they are ranked based on:
1. Head-to-head record between the tied teams.
2. Head-to-head point difference.
3. Overall point difference in all regular season games.
4. Overall points scored in all regular season games.

## 5. BASIC GAME RULES
* Game Duration: 4 quarters of 10 minutes each (total 40 minutes).
* Overtime (OT): If the score is tied, 5-minute overtime periods are played until a winner is determined.
* Shot Clock: 24 seconds (resets to 14 seconds after an offensive rebound).
* Foul Out: A player is disqualified from the game after committing 5 personal fouls.
* Team Bonus: Teams enter the bonus (free throws for every foul) after the 4th team foul in each quarter.
