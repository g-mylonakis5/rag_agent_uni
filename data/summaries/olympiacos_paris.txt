The Greeks broke the visitors in a fourth quarter, who were left without Nadir Hifi before the match
Olympiacos achieved victory in the 12th round of the EuroLeague, defeating Paris on their home court with a score of 98-86.
Justin Robinson played one of the best games of the season in Europe, capitalizing on the absence of Nadir Hifi, who missed the match in Piraeus due to a minor injury.
Robinson scored 35 points (7/10 from three), but even that wasn’t enough. Alongside him, Jeremy Morgan scored in double figures with 13 points.
For the winners, who played without Frank Ntilikina, Keenan Evans, and Moustapha Fall, Evan Fournier scored 18 points, Sasha Vezenkov finished the game with 13 points (4/15 from the field), while Tyler Dorsey and Tyson Ward each had 12.
The visitors had the lead for most of the first half and seemed poised to bring the game to a tense finish. However, the Greeks went on a run in the fourth quarter, pulling away to a significant lead and eventually securing the victory.
Olympiacos now has eight wins and four losses, and they will seek a ninth victory when they visit Crvena Zvezda. On the other hand, Paris has a record of 5-7 and will visit Dubai in the next round.
