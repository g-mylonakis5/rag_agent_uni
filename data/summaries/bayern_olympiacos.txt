The team in red and white maintained the lead from the start of the match and, with as many as ten scorers, secured the victory
Olympiakos recorded their fourth win in the EuroLeague this season, defeating Bayern 96-71 in Munich.
Tyler Dorsey started the game well and finished in the same rhythm, being the top scorer for the visiting team with 19 points. Sasha Vezenkov added 18 points and five rebounds, while Donta Hall contributed a solid performance with 11 points and eight rebounds.
On the other side, Andreas Obst scored 15 points, and Oskar da Silva had 12 in a match where fans still haven’t seen Spencer Dinwiddie, the latest addition.
Olympiakos practically resolved everything in the first half. The difference was already double-digit in the first quarter, reaching 19 points by halftime, and in the second half in Munich, only the final score was awaited, which ended up being 96-71.
After six rounds, the Greek team is at a 4-2 record and will pursue their fifth win in Piraeus against Monaco, in a rematch of last year’s semifinal. On the other hand, Bayern remains with two victories and will now face Real Madrid in front of their fans.
