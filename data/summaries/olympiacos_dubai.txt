Olympiacos cruised at the first EuroLeague home game of the season
Sasha Vezenkov and Nikola Milutinov shone as Olympiacos showcased its strength against EuroLeague newcomers Dubai BC.
The Piraeus team defended its home court in style, taking down Jurica Golemac’s side with a final score of 86-67, improving to 2–1 in the EuroLeague standings.
Vezenkov dominated with 25 points and 11 rebounds for a game-high PIR of 34, while Milutinov also recorded a double-double with 13 points and 11 rebounds for a PIR of 25.
Tyler Dorsey followed with 14 points, while Saben Lee added 10. Frank Ntilikina contributed seven points and three assists in his EuroLeague debut for Olympiacos. It’s worth noting that coach Georgios Bartzokas did not have Evan Fournier and Thomas Walkup at his disposal.
As for Dubai, former Olympiacos player Filip Petrusev scored 14 points, Dwayne Bacon followed with 13, while Davis Bertans and Mfiondu Kabengele added 10 points each.
Olympiacos steadily increased its lead throughout the game, going up 23–16 after the first quarter and 43–31 at halftime. Vezenkov dominated in the third period, pushing his team to a 71–52 advantage, which proved enough for a comfortable home win.
