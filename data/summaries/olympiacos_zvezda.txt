A very strange match was played in Piraeus, where Georgios Bartzokas’ team held a double-digit lead for most of the game. However, by the end of the first half, the visitors had closed the gap, entering the second half with only a four-point deficit (49-45).
Several times, the home team was close to pulling away and settling the game, but Zvezda kept coming back. In the end, the Serbian team had a few chances to push the match into a full-blown drama, but Sasa Obradovic‘s players missed a significant number of crucial shots and lost some possessions in key moments. As a result, Olympiacos maintained their lead and ultimately secured the victory.
