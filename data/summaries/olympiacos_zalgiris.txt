The Reds got an easy win, however they suffered a big loss
Olympiacos tied Zalgiris Kaunas and Crvena Zvezda Meridianbet in the second spot of the EuroLeague standings after beating the Lithuanians 95-78, but nobody wanted to celebrate after.
Keenan Evans returned to action, only to get injured again after two minutes.
Still, Olympiacos got the job done with ease, as Tyler Dorsey scored 30 points and Nikola Milutinov added 16 points and eight rebounds.
Sylvain Francisco had 15 points, and former Olympiacos player Moses Wright added 13 points and 10 rebounds for Zalgiris, which couldn’t keep up with the Greeks.
The game was a one-sided affair, with Olympiacos leading by 22 points (36-14) after 13 minutes.
Zalgiris trimmed the lead to 11 (47-36) with a three by Maodo Lo, but Olympiacos answered back with three free throws by Saben Lee to close the first half up by 14 (50-36) and then extended the distance to 23 points (61-38) with an 11-2 run.
The score at the end of the third was 74-53, and the game was over.
